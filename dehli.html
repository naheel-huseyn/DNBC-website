Business

Delhi's business scene is booming with the Delhi Stock Exchange reporting a steady rise. Startups in fintech and clean energy are attracting international investment. The Delhi government has also rolled out incentives for MSMEs to boost economic growth and innovation across NCR.

Sports

Delhi Capitals pulled off a thrilling win in the IPL with a final-over sixer. Meanwhile, school-level sports tournaments are gaining popularity, and preparations for the Commonwealth trials are underway with upgraded facilities across the capital.

Food

Delhi’s street food festival in Chandni Chowk saw record attendance, offering iconic dishes from across the country. Michelin-starred chefs also participated in events promoting fusion cuisines, giving Delhi’s food scene a global twist.

Weather

Heavy rainfall is expected throughout the week as monsoon winds intensify. The Meteorological Department has issued alerts for localized flooding. Residents are advised to avoid low-lying areas and monitor updates closely.

Education

Delhi University and JNU are launching new interdisciplinary programs, especially in AI and sustainability. Government schools are adopting smart-class technologies with tablets and training teachers in digital literacy to enhance learning outcomes.

Current Affairs

The Delhi Assembly is debating major reforms in traffic regulation and waste management. Anti-pollution measures are also in focus with proposals for car rationing and stricter industrial emissions norms.

Entertainment

The International Film Festival of Delhi kicked off with global entries and red carpet glamour. Local theaters are also staging classical dramas while new-age short films are gaining traction among youth on OTT platforms.

Health

AIIMS and other hospitals report declining COVID-19 cases. Focus now shifts to mental health and community wellness, with free check-up camps and yoga programs launched in collaboration with NGOs.

Technology

Delhi’s tech parks are hosting innovation expos featuring AI, robotics, and green tech. Startups are booming with VC funding, and coding bootcamps are now common across major institutions preparing youth for the digital economy.

Lifestyle

Delhiites are embracing a blend of tradition and modernity with a rise in eco-conscious living, boutique fitness studios, and ethnic fashion. Weekend flea markets, art exhibitions, and café culture are redefining urban living in the capital.
